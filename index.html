About the Project

This project focuses on creating awareness about air pollution and promoting eco-friendly practices to reduce harmful emissions. Our team has conducted surveys, awareness campaigns, and plantation drives to support cleaner air initiatives.

Main Causes of Air Pollution

  • Vehicle emissions 🚗
  • Industrial smoke 🏭
  • Burning of waste 🔥
  • Deforestation 🌳
  • Household chemicals 🧴

Steps to Reduce Air Pollution

🌿 Plant more trees
🚲 Use public transport or bicycles
🌞 Switch to renewable energy
🧹 Avoid burning plastic and waste
♻️ Recycle and reuse materials
